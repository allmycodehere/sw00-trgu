Space Planning.

Space Planning is the process of organizing furniture and office functions to work effectively together while using space efficiently. Consider the workgroup function the building codes and regulations, lighting, teaming requirements, inter-communication, and storage to make the best use of available space. Other factors, such as the use of technology – computer, double monitors, racks etc., have to be built into the plan for each office workspace.

Window Treatments

Window treatments help define the style and mood of a room. When deciding on a window treatment start with the style, then think about the color and design and make hardware choices to add the right finishing touch. Sqft Interior helps you to first step in choosing a window treatment that decides how much privacy and light control you need. For example: For a room in which minimal privacy is needed, maximize the available natural light and select sheer fabrics. For more privacy and a cozy effect, create a soft wall of opaque texture and rich color. However, beware of overdressing windows with yards of fabric, which can make your room look dated.
